Morning Glow Magic Detox Tea: Lemon and Lime Recipe for Glowing Skin and Energy

Morning Glow Magic Detox Tea is a refreshing herbal blend crafted to awaken your body from within. Packed with antioxidants from ingredients like ginger, turmeric, lemon balm, dandelion, and rooibos, it promotes glowing skin by combating oxidative stress, supporting collagen production, and helping reduce inflammation for a brighter, more radiant complexion.

For gut health, its gentle detoxifying herbs aid digestion, soothe bloating, and support the liver and natural elimination processes, leaving you feeling lighter and more balanced.

It also delivers natural energy rejuvenation with a caffeine-free (or low-caffeine) lift that boosts metabolism and vitality without the crash, helping you start the day with clarity and sustained focus.

Sip it warm each morning as part of your wellness ritual for inside-out glow and renewed energy.

Ingredients (for 1 large mug or 1 liter pitcher)

1 tsp green tea leaves, for energy and skin antioxidants.

Half tsp dried ginger (or 2 to 3 fresh slices), for digestion support.

Half tsp turmeric powder (or fresh) plus a pinch of black pepper, for glow and anti-inflammatory benefits.

Half tsp peppermint leaves, for bloating relief.

1 tsp hibiscus or rosehip, for a vitamin C boost for skin.

A very small piece of fresh organic garlic (about 1/8 clove, thinly sliced or crushed), for gut-cleansing and immunity.

2 to 3 slices of fresh lemon and/or lime (organic preferred), which replace juice for a milder, aromatic citrus flavor and ongoing infusion.

Optional: a pinch of cinnamon and 1 tsp honey or maple syrup.

How to Make It

Add green tea leaves, ginger, turmeric, peppermint, hibiscus or rosehip, the organic garlic piece, and the lemon and/or lime slices to your teapot or mug.

Pour hot water (80C / 175F).

Steep 6 to 8 minutes for full citrus infusion.

Strain (or leave slices in for continued flavor) and sweeten if desired.

Enjoy warm as your morning ritual.

Why the Lemon and Lime Slices Work

Using slices gives a gentler, more fragrant citrus note that infuses beautifully and looks pretty in the cup. It still delivers vitamin C and detox support without the sharpness of straight juice.
