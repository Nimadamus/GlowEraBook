The Book

A love letter to the woman rebuilding herself

Glow Era isn't another rulebook on how to be perfect. It's permission, permission to slow down, to soften, to choose yourself without apology. Prudence Nteo blends warm storytelling with practical rituals to help you rebuild your relationship with your body, your mind, and your worth.

Across every page, Glow Era walks you through the quiet, powerful work of self-care: setting boundaries without guilt, healing old wounds, building unshakeable confidence, and creating daily rituals that actually stick. This is the book for anyone who has poured into everyone else and forgotten to fill their own cup.

The Author

Prudence Nteo

Prudence Nteo writes from lived experience, the messy, beautiful process of learning to choose herself. Her voice is warm, honest, and free of judgment, like a wise friend who's already walked the path and is holding the door open for you.

Through Glow Era, Prudence shares the mindset shifts, rituals, and hard-won lessons that helped her rebuild her confidence from the inside out, so you can do the same, on your own timeline, in your own way.
